Our services

Product

We can help you develop your products. If you’re launching a new product we can help accelerate the process as well as bring some external viewpoint to challenge existing assumptions. We can also bring some extra executing power to scale an existing product or to build your product roadmap.

Growth

We work on new country expansion, B2B sales expansion plan (identifying priority targets, designing sales playbook), funnel review (identifying leakages and improving conversion) and partnership identification and set up

MVP

You might want to quickly set up an MVP as you are still early in your fundraising process and do not have yet the full range of tech capabilities. Alternatively, your teams might be fully focused on evolving the core product, not leaving enough resources to test new waters. We can help with both, bringing to bear a team of experienced developers, tech leads and product specialists

Headhunting

We can help identify top talents for non-tech positions: Product, Sales, Chief of Staff, Ops, … We understand the needs of a tech company and can tap into our large network of talents.

BP

We can help you work on your BP, harmonize the view between your leadership team, challenge the financial forecast and build a growth story for your next round of funding

Corporate development

We provide you with experienced professionals to conduct the due diligences and PMI of potential targets
